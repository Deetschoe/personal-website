Why?

My motivation to start a business or simply make money originated from my burning desire to travel to Japan. Knowing that long-distance travel can be quite costly, I dedicated myself to the mission of saving up as much money as possible to transform my dream into reality. After three years of relentless hard work, during my senior year when I graduated high school early, I finally embarked on the journey with two of my high school friends, successfully achieving my goal. It may sound unconventional, but it worked wonders for me.

Now, my motivation has changed, focusing on two core aspects: gamifying wealth creation and embracing my never ending curiosity. Once you surpass what most people consider achievable you can't go back... Also id like to add, there's an incredible sense of fulfillment in giving guidance to fellow entrepreneurs who are starting their own journeys!
