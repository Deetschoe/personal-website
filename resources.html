So, for the last few years, I have been asked to provide links to some books or creators I watch, from whom I can get advice or whom I look up to.

Youtubers

Jayhoovy

I love this guys content, he is very transparent about how he is evolving his company and growing good culture.

Alex Hormozi

This guy is truly inspiring! His content has evolved so much, and he makes complex concepts easy to understand.

YCombinator

Even though they are a startup incubator, they post awesome content and interviews. If you're into the startup world, you gotta check them out!

Their interviews are top-notch! They ask deep questions and put out high-quality stuff.

My First Million

This channel is a treasure trove for all things entrepreneurship-related.

Naval

Take a listen to his "How to Get Rich" YouTube video, life-changing.

Books

Rich dad poor dad (Robert Kiyosaki)

8/10, Talks about the importance of financial literacy and independence. You will get a better perspective on wealth in this book and how to build it.

The Four Agreements (Don Miguel Ruiz)

10/10, My favorite book when it comes to wisdom its a classic.

Rhinoceros Success (Scott Alexander)

6/10, Comedical book about not caring what people think and acting like a rhinoceros; tough skin; able to handle situations better than others.

Atomic Habits (James Clear)

10/10, This book is perfect for anyone struggling to stick to their habits and how to get rid of bad habits it's a must read

The Subtle Art Of Not Giving A FxCK (Mark Manson)

9/10, I loved the back story in this book I feel like I learned something new about not caring what people think about me and I feel like everyone should read this book in order to learn that

The Almanack Of Naval Ravikant (Naval Ravikant, Eric Jorgenson)

100/10, Holy crap anyone that doesn't read this book is missing out on so much valuable information, Some people like this book, and others don't like it but I LOVED this book. It is my favorite book of all time, This book is filled with Naval's insights about the future of work and wealth.

$100M Offers (Alex Hormozi)

10/10, One of my favorite creators on the internet that I've learned so much from is Alex Hormozi, this book talks about creating a grand slam offer for your business and so much more! I highly suggest this book to anyone wanting to start their own business and succeed.

12 Months to $1 Million (Ryan Daniel Moran)

7/10, Improve your understanding of scaling your business with this book. I thoroughly enjoyed reading it—it was both entertaining and highly informative. However, by the time I read it, I had already known most of the information. But it's an excellent resource for beginners!

Essentialism (Greg Mckeown)

10/10, this book is about essentialism, the pursuit of less but better. I recommend this book to anyone who struggles with saying no to opportunity’s and if you want to get better at picking better opportunity's to change your life

​ Start with why (Simon Sinek)

6/10, If you don't know your why in life, I recommend you to read this book but if you already have that "why", I would read a different book.

The Slight Edge (Jeff Olson)

9/10, I really enjoyed this book and the author's story, He opened my eyes to the slight edge and how to make it work for you.

So Good They Can't Ignore You (Cal Newport)

10/10, I was not expecting this book to be so good, Mr. Newport opened my eyes and made me rethink my way of doing business solely based on passion. I realized if you get good enough it will become your passion and so much more! I highly recommend this book

Psycho-Cybernetics (Maxwell Maltz)

7/10, I would have loved for this book to be one of the first books I read but after consuming so many books I found this book to be boring in all honesty. Yes, it has a ton of great info and can truly change your life but a lot of the information I have heard about before but I still highly suggest you, check this book out especially if you're just getting into the entrepreneur space.

The Road Less Stupid (Keith J. Cunningham)

10/10, I was not expecting this book to have so much great info on business and thinking on decisions. Please check this book out once you get the chance
